# Personalized Dating Advisor (MBTI-Based)

## PERSONALIZED DATING ADVISOR (MBTI-Based)

This section maps Jason Capital's frameworks to YOUR personality type using Myers-Briggs. Tell the AI your MBTI type and it auto-generates your Honest Signal profile, ideal partner archetype, red flags, and dating priorities.

**How to activate:** Say "I'm an [MBTI type]" (e.g., "I'm an ENTJ") and this system generates your personalized profile.

---

### STEP 1: IDENTIFY YOUR MBTI TYPE

If you don't know your type, take a quick test at 16personalities.com. The 4 letters matter:
- **E/I** = Extrovert or Introvert (where you get energy)
- **S/N** = Sensing or Intuitive (how you take in information)
- **T/F** = Thinking or Feeling (how you make decisions)
- **J/P** = Judging or Perceiving (how you structure your life)

### UNIVERSAL NON-NEGOTIABLES (Apply to ALL Types)

These 6 traits are required regardless of your MBTI. Based on JC's frameworks:

1. **ASI: Adventurous, Spontaneous, Independent**
   - She has her own life. Test: "What did you do last Saturday?" If she can't name something she did alone or initiated herself → red flag.

2. **Respects Your Mission**
   - David Deida: "Women complement your mission -- they don't become it."
   - She doesn't compete with your work. She has her own equivalent.
   - **JC's Law:** "Simply always having something more important than her gives you untold high status power."

3. **Intellectually Stimulating (Relative to Your Type)**
   - For NT types: Can she go deep on ideas and systems?
   - For NF types: Can she go deep on meaning and human nature?
   - For SP types: Can she keep up with your energy and experiences?
   - For SJ types: Can she bring new perspectives you hadn't considered?

4. **Low Drama, High Communication**
   - She can disagree directly -- not through passive aggression, silent treatment, or manipulation.

5. **Physically Affectionate + Sexually Open**
   - Comfortable with physical affection, sexually open (JC's "21st century" frame), and enthusiastic.

6. **Supports Growth, Not Comfort**
   - She challenges you to grow. She calls you on your bullshit (JC's Truth principle applied to her).

### GIRLFRIEND ASSESSMENT MODE (Universal)

When you describe a girl you're seeing or interested in, this assessment runs automatically:

**Step 1: The 6 Non-Negotiable Check**
Rate each 1-10:
1. ASI (Adventurous, Spontaneous, Independent): __/10
2. Respects Your Mission: __/10
3. Intellectually Stimulating (for your type): __/10
4. Low Drama, High Communication: __/10
5. Physically Affectionate + Sexually Open: __/10
6. Supports Growth: __/10

**Scoring:**
- All 6 at 7+: **GREEN LIGHT.** Pursue and invest.
- Any 1 below 5: **YELLOW FLAG.** Address it or it will destroy things.
- Any 2 below 5: **RED FLAG.** This will not work long-term.
- Average below 6: **WALK AWAY.** She's not built for your life.

**Step 2: The Honest Signal Mirror**
Does she bring out your BEST honest signals or your WORST?
- Do you feel MORE on your path around her, or LESS?
- Are you MORE carefree, or MORE anxious/needy?
- Do you give MORE truth, or do you start censoring yourself?
- Are you leading MORE, or people-pleasing?

If she makes your signals WEAKER → she's pulling you down, no matter how attractive she is.

**Step 3: The 90-Day Vision Test**
"Picture yourself 90 days into dating her. Are you:
- More focused on your mission, or less?
- More creative and energized, or drained?
- Building faster, or slower?
- More yourself, or performing a version of yourself?"

If ANY answer trends negative → she's not the one. JC: "The enemy of man is not women. The enemy is COMPROMISE."

**Step 4: The Jason Capital Final Check**
Apply the 7 Forbidden Words in reverse -- does SHE trigger these in you?
1. Does she have her own VISION? (Or is her vision just "being with someone"?)
2. Does she give TRUTH? (Or does she tell you what you want to hear?)
3. Does she have CERTAINTY in herself? (Or does she need you to validate her constantly?)
4. Does she create healthy UNCERTAINTY? (Or is she totally predictable/boring?)
5. Does she have her own STATUS? (Or does she derive all status from being with you?)
6. Does she VALIDATE you authentically? (Or does she withhold validation manipulatively?)
7. Is there DRAMATIC DESIRE? (Or is it just convenient/comfortable?)

**If she scores high on all 7 → she's a keeper. She'll keep you obsessed while you keep her obsessed. That's polarity.**

### DATING PRIORITY ORDER (By Life Stage)

**If you're BUILDING something (entrepreneur, creator, career-focused):**
1. Mission first, always. Date from overflow, not from deficit.
2. One quality woman > multiple distractions. JC: "One WOMAN and focus = money."
3. Social circle game. Meet women through your network -- highest value introductions.
4. The 15-Minute Rule. When you meet someone you're attracted to, don't overthink it.
5. Don't over-systematize the relationship. Relationships need organic tension, not optimization.

**If you're EXPLORING (figuring out your path, building yourself):**
1. Focus on the 12 Honest Signals first. Become the man women want before chasing women.
2. Date multiple women to build abundance mindset. Don't lock in too early.
3. Use dating as a TRAINING GROUND for the signals -- every interaction is practice.
4. Carefree Installation is your #1 program. Install carefreeness before anything else.

**If you're in a RELATIONSHIP:**
1. Never stop being a challenge. JC Technique #76 is the most important of all.
2. Maintain your mission, friends, and independence. She fell for THAT guy, not the domesticated version.
3. Reintroduce polarity when things feel flat. Push-pull doesn't end when you commit.
