## THE SEX TEST (How to Tell if She's Open to Having Sex with You)

**Source**: The Sex Test - Jason Capital (Audio Training / Webinar Bonus)

### Core Premise (Write This Down)

> "She will not talk about sex with you unless she's open to experimenting or doing it with you."

She won't talk about it if she's not open to doing it with you. Period.

### Origin Story

JC at a pool party at the Capital Compound. 25 girls, 5-6 friends. Talking to a girl who was a 5/6 -- nice girl, but not attractive enough for him. She kept trying to bring up sexual things: people hooking up, stories about fucking, her views on blowjobs. Every time she brought up sex, JC clammed up. Refused to talk about it. Waited until she changed the subject.

**Why he clammed up**: He didn't want to drop any hints. Didn't want her to think he was interested sexually.

**The realization**: He asked female friends if this was universal. They ALL confirmed: "We talk about sex with guys we're comfortable with sexually. If a guy is creepy or we're not interested, we won't talk about it AT ALL because we don't want to drop any hints."

### The Two Modes: Sexual Guy vs. Horny Guy

**YOU WANT TO BE SEXUAL GUY, NOT HORNY GUY.**

The more she talks about sex with you, the more likely it's going to happen. But if you want her to STOP talking about it, stop being cool. Be needy. Go low status.

#### Sexual Guy Signals:
1. **High-Status Body Language** (THE STEAK of the meal)
   - Leaning back. Shoulders back. Everything back. Chilled back.
   - "When in doubt, lean out" (from Higher Status)
   - Nonchalant. Indifferent. Unattached body language.

2. **Talk About Sex Like Eating Ice Cream** (THE SIDES)
   - Describe the EXPERIENCE of the act using sensual terms
   - JC's ice cream example: "I took a deep scoop... gently laid it down... licked the cold spoon with the tip of my tongue... the first lick was delicious... slowly dug my spoon deeper..."
   - That's how you talk about sexual things -- sensual, experiential
   - NOT: "I stuck a pinky in her ass and made her squeal" -- that's HORNY GUY talk

3. **Multi-Thread (Don't Force the Sex Topic)**
   - If she changes the subject while you're talking about sex, GO WITH IT
   - If she brings up something random and you say "wait wait, what about blowjobs? Let's go back to that" -- that signals NEEDINESS
   - Signals you're not used to talking about sex with girls
   - Signals desperate, not abundant
   - REFUSING TO MULTI-THREAD = #1 tell you're horny guy, not sexual guy

### The Test Close (How to Bring Up Sex)

**Don't ask directly**: "What's your favorite position?" / "When's the last time you had sex?"

**Instead, use the environment:**
1. Find a couple sitting at a table
2. Talk about their body language for a second
3. Make a cold read connecting their body language to their sex life
4. Example: "You can totally tell the way they're standing right now... they probably haven't fucked in like three weeks"

**Why this works**: You brought up sex with ZERO pressure. Not talking about her, not about you, not about her-and-you. Talking about other people. It's a TEST CLOSE.

**If she's comfortable talking about those other people's sex life with you** → she'll be comfortable talking about sex with you → she's open to sex with you.

### From Test Close to Personal Value Statement

After the environmental sex talk, make a PERSONAL VALUE STATEMENT:

> "See, and that's why for me, sex is so important in the relationship. Like, if you're not having sex or doing something sexual with your partner every single day, I just feel disconnected from her."

- This is high-status: stating YOUR values openly, honestly, authentically
- She then shares something about HER sex life or relationships
- Now you're both talking about sex comfortably
- You're leaning back, kicking it, and you KNOW: if she's talking about sex with you, she's open to sex with you

### Integration with Situation Room

When analyzing text conversations or screenshots:
- **If she brings up anything sexual** → GREEN LIGHT. She's open. Be Sexual Guy.
- **If she avoids all sexual topics** → She's not there yet. Build more attraction first.
- **If she changed the subject when YOU brought up sex** → She's testing. Don't force it. Multi-thread. Come back to it later naturally.
- **If she's talking about other people's sex lives with you** → She's comfortable. Escalate with a personal value statement.

## HOW TO GET HOTTER WOMEN (The 11 Secrets to Attracting 8's, 9's and 10's)

**Source**: How to Get Hotter Women by Jason Capital (Book, First Print Edition, Copyright 2011)

### Core Philosophy

8's, 9's and 10's are not just about looks -- personality plays a big role too. Girls who look incredible AND are incredible to be around. Everything that works on "normal" girls works on hot girls too -- it's just AMPLIFIED and EXACERBATED.

**Three things you must understand:**
1. **Her Mindset** -- What's going on in her head (status, perception, insecurity)
2. **Her Perception Of You** -- How you carry yourself, communicate, how others see you
3. **Your Perception Of You** -- Purely knowing you're the fucking shit, 'cause you are

### The "Me!" Exercise

Write your name at the top of a page. Below it, describe the EXACT version of yourself you WANT to be. Not who you are now -- who you WANT to be. What's he look like? How does he dress? How do people look at him? How do women look at him? What does he say? Write EVERYTHING.

### Secret #1: Dominant Body Language

Body language = 55% of communication (tonality 38%, words 7%). The majority of your focus should be on body language.

**Truth**: Women are attracted to high-status (a guy of higher-status than they see themselves as).

**The 5 Steps to Dominant Body Language:**

**A. Slower Movements**
- Cut the speed of every movement you make IN HALF, now
- Standing up? Twice as slow. Sitting down? Twice as slow
- Twitchy/reactive/fidgety = nervous, anxious, "beta", not in control
- "This might be the most important thing you can take away from this book"

**B. Take Up More Space**
- Open up your legs, lean back, spread your arms
- Don't be "small" -- just take up and own your space
- People will "get it" and won't fight you for space

**C. Leaning Away As They're Leaning In**
- Create the body language perspective that SHE'S COMING TO YOU
- You lean back, she leans toward you = looks like she's chasing/pursuing you
- This actually transfers to her head: "wow, I must be attracted to this guy"

**D. Eye Contact Rules**
- **When YOU'RE talking**: strong, relaxed eye contact 80-85% of the time
- **When SHE'S talking**: strong relaxed eye contact 50-60% of the time
- This shows: (1) you believe in what you have to say, (2) she has to EARN your attention
- When looking away from her while she talks, be subtle -- look at something interesting behind her

**E. Crotch Central**
- Show and bring attention to your crotch
- Spread legs apart when sitting, big wide stances
- Hands around belt near crotch (a la James Dean)
- Communicates: true man, incredibly confident, incredibly sexual

### Secret #2: Tonality

A VOICE that people WANT to hear. Good rhythm. Deeper voice. Variations in volume and cadence.

**JC's Tonality Breakdown (Storytelling Example):**
- "That's awesome" → plain tonality, not impressed
- "...you know," → slight PAUSE to create suspense/tension (higher perceived value)
- "crazy" → extra emphasis (trigger word, shock value, sets tone)
- "I was in DC" → pause after, let "I travel" marinate
- "...for a marketing event" → embedded content (shows ambition)
- "who I almost NEVER get to see" → volume UP on "NEVER" then immediately drops quieter
- "we decided" → emphasis + short pause, then "good time" → voice DEEPENS, lengthens to "GOOOOD"
- Numbers/lists → pause between items, creates open loops
- "you won't believe" → slow down, lower voice, pause before reveal

**Key Principle**: Use strategic pausing to create tension and perceived value. Vary rhythm between normal baseline, louder emphasis, and quiet/slow moments.

## THE TEXTING GOD SYSTEM (Jason Capital's Approach to Digital Communication)

**Source**: Synthesized from ALL JC frameworks (Always Know What to Say, 50 Questions, Attraction God, Sex Test, Higher Status, 77 Ways, Make Women Want You) -- rebuilt as the JC VERSION of texting/DMs. This replaces generic "copy-paste script" approaches with JC's identity-first, principle-based system.

### JC's Texting Philosophy (The Foundation)

> "Texting is LOGISTICS, not seduction. Your job over text is to get her in front of you. The real game happens in person."

**The 4 Rules of JC Texting:**
1. **Be the Prize** -- Your texts should communicate that you have a full, exciting life and she's lucky to be part of it. Never text like you're trying to earn her attention.
2. **Less > More** -- The guy who texts less has more power. Your messages should be SHORT, FUN, and PURPOSEFUL. No paragraphs. No novels. No explaining yourself.
3. **Multi-Thread, Never Force** -- If a topic dies, LET IT DIE. Move to something else. Forcing a conversation thread = neediness signal (directly from the Sex Test principle).
4. **Mirror Her Investment** -- Match her energy, never exceed it. If she sends 3 words, you send 3-5. If she sends a paragraph, you can send more. NEVER out-invest her.

### The Status Text Framework (How JC Would Actually Text)

Instead of copy-paste scripts, JC gives you FRAMEWORKS you adapt to your own voice:

#### PHASE 1: THE OPENER (First Message)

**Principle**: Your opener should do ONE thing -- make her curious enough to respond. Not impress her. Not compliment her. CREATE INTRIGUE.

**JC-Approved Opener Types:**

| Type | Example | Why It Works |
|------|---------|-------------|
| **Snap Cold Read** | "You look like trouble. The fun kind though." | Signals you READ people, creates intrigue |
| **Observation + Tease** | "That [specific thing in her profile] tells me you're either really adventurous or really good at pretending" | Shows you actually looked at HER, not just her face |
| **Assumption Stack** | "Something tells me you're the type who says 'let's go' to plans without asking where" | Me/You communication -- makes her qualify/deny |
| **Challenge Opener** | "You seem cool but I can't tell if you're actually fun or just photogenic" | Fun + Challenge in one line |
| **The Simple Direct** | "You caught my eye. What's your deal?" | High status directness -- no games, no tricks |

**JC Would NEVER:**
- Send "hey" or "hi" or "what's up"
- Compliment her looks as an opener (she hears this 50x/day)
- Send anything longer than 2 sentences as opener
- Use a pre-written script she's seen from other guys
- Reply to her Story with heart-eyes (low status fan behavior)

#### PHASE 2: THE VIBE (Building Connection Over Text)

**Principle**: Use Me/You Communication. Everything is "we" and "us" energy. Make it feel like you two are already a team against the boring world.

**JC Texting Tools:**

1. **The Cold Read Text**: "I already know you're the type who [assumption]." She'll either confirm or deny -- both create conversation.

2. **Push-Pull Texts**: "You're actually pretty funny for someone with such terrible taste in [thing from her profile]" -- compliment + tease in one message.

3. **The Thread Game**: Instead of Q&A (boring), play a text game:
   - "Okay rapid fire. Tacos or sushi? Mountains or beach? Best lie you've ever told?"
   - This is from JC's "Always Know What to Say" adapted to text

4. **Statement > Question**: Instead of "What do you do for fun?" → "You seem like you do something creative. Am I right?" (Statements create intrigue; questions create interviews)

5. **The Callback**: Reference something from earlier in the conversation as an inside joke. This creates "we have a thing" energy.

**Investment Ratio Rule**: Your texts should be slightly LESS invested than hers. If she's writing full sentences, you write slightly shorter ones. If she starts writing more, you can match. NEVER chase the conversation.

#### PHASE 3: THE PIVOT (Getting Off the App / Getting the Date)

**Principle**: Move fast. Don't become her text buddy. The goal is ALWAYS to meet in person. JC's 15-Minute Rule adapted to text: Don't text for more than a day without proposing to meet.

**JC-Approved Number/Date Moves:**

1. **The Assumptive Close**: "We should grab a drink. What's your number?" -- Not asking permission. Stating what's happening.

2. **The Challenge Close**: "You seem fun but I can only tell so much over text. Let's see if you're actually this cool in person. Thursday or Friday?"

3. **The Enticing Offer**: "I just found this [specific cool place]. You'd love it. Come with me [day]." -- Leading, specific, not asking.

4. **The Takeaway Close**: "I'm deleting this app this weekend. But you seem like someone I should know. Shoot me your number before I disappear." (Uses scarcity + decisiveness)

5. **The Direct**: "I'm not a pen-pal. Let's actually hang. What's your schedule look like this week?"

**JC Would NEVER:**
- Ask "would you maybe want to hang out sometime?" (weak, no specifics)
- Text for weeks without proposing to meet
- Let HER pick the plan (you LEAD)
- Say "I'd love to take you out" (pedestalizing)

#### PHASE 4: THE CONFIRMATION (Anti-Flake System)

**Principle**: Flaking happens because she doesn't feel enough TENSION or INVESTMENT. Your confirmation text should build anticipation, not beg for confirmation.

**JC Anti-Flake Texts:**

1. **The Tease Confirmation** (day before): "Fair warning -- I'm way more fun in person. Don't fall in love too fast tomorrow ;)"

2. **The Takeaway Confirmation** (day of): "Still down for tonight? If not, no sweat -- my backup plan involves nachos and zero small talk"

3. **The Assumptive Confirmation** (day of): "Wearing something cute. See you at 8." -- Doesn't even ask. Just assumes.

4. **The Callback Confirmation**: Reference your earlier conversation: "I've been thinking about your terrible take on [earlier topic]. Need to debate this in person. See you at 8."

**If she flakes**: ONE chance. "No worries. Let me know when you're free." Then RADIO SILENCE. She reaches out or she doesn't. Never chase a flake.

#### PHASE 5: SEXUAL ESCALATION OVER TEXT (The Sex Test Method, Digitized)

**Principle**: From the Sex Test -- she won't talk about sex unless she's open to it with you. Your job is to CREATE the opening, not force it. Be SEXUAL GUY, not HORNY GUY.

**The JC Sexual Escalation Ladder (Over Text):**

**Step 1 -- The Seed**: Plant a small sexual reference that's ABOUT OTHER PEOPLE, not you and her.
- "My friend just told me the most ridiculous story about her date last night... I'm dying"
- "People who are boring in conversation are definitely boring in [pause] ...other areas too"
- If she engages → she's open. If she changes subject → not ready yet.

**Step 2 -- The Value Statement**: Share YOUR values about intimacy (from the Sex Test)
- "For me, if there's no chemistry when I'm with someone, what's the point? Life's too short for boring connections"
- "I think most people settle for mediocre everything -- mediocre dates, mediocre conversations, mediocre... everything"

**Step 3 -- The Sensual Tease**: Talk about experiences SENSUALLY, like eating ice cream (directly from JC's method)
- Describe a great meal, a sunset, a travel moment with sensory language
- "The kind of night where you don't even check your phone once"
- If she matches your energy → she's into it

**Step 4 -- The Direct Tension**: Only after Steps 1-3 have landed

**NEVER DO:**

- Continue sex talk if she changes the subject (multi-thread, don't force)

### The "Tell Me Exactly What to Say" Decision Tree

When you paste a conversation into the Situation Room, here's how JC would analyze it:

**Step 1: Assess Her Investment Level**
- Is she asking questions back? → High investment → You can be more direct
- One-word answers? → Low investment → Switch to statements and cold reads
- She's texting first? → Very high investment → She's yours to lose. Lead.
- No response? → Zero investment → One follow-up max, then silence

**Step 2: Identify Where You Are**
- Just matched / first message? → PHASE 1 (Opener)
- Back and forth but no number? → PHASE 3 (Pivot to number/date)
- Have her number but no date? → PHASE 3 (Pivot to date)
- Date is set? → PHASE 4 (Anti-flake)
- Went on a date, now texting? → PHASE 5 (Escalation) or back to PHASE 3 (next date)

**Step 3: Apply the Right Framework**
Based on Phase, use the corresponding tools above. ALWAYS err on the side of:
- Shorter messages
- More challenging/fun tone
- Moving toward meeting in person
- Being the one to end conversations first

**Step 4: The JC Gut Check**
Before sending ANY text, ask:
1. Would a high-status guy with 5 other options send this? (If no → delete it)
2. Am I REACTING to her or LEADING the conversation? (Always lead)
3. Is this moving us toward meeting in person? (If not → why am I sending it?)
4. Does this sound like FUN + CHALLENGE? (If it's just nice/boring → rewrite)

### Integration with Situation Room
