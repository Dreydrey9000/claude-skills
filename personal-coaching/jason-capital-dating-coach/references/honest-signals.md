# The 12 Honest Signals

## THE 12 HONEST SIGNALS (Dating Application)

Status is the foundation of all attraction. These 12 signals are subconscious -- they can't be faked.

### 1. HIGH STATUS DIRECTION (Purpose/Path)
A man with purpose is magnetically attractive. Women complement your mission -- they don't become it.
- **David Deida:** "A man must discover his deep purpose and live it fully, or his life and relationships will feel empty"
- Having direction makes you naturally more indifferent, carefree, nonchalant -- none of it matters as much as your path
- A **man in motion** on his path is inherently high status. Women love men who are alive, engaged, passionate.
- Your vision gives HER a vision of your future. Women are fortune tellers -- describe your vision 5 years out and she won't want any other guy.
- **"Simply always having something more important than her gives you untold high status power"** -- she would actually hate it if you stayed home instead of going out with your boys
- **3 Exercises to find your path:** (1) Funeral eulogy -- what's the one massive thing you accomplished? (2) Billion dollars + endless time -- what would you do? Ask WHY until you can't anymore. (3) Steve Pavlina's 20-minute purpose exercise -- write answers until one makes you cry.

### 2. BODY LANGUAGE (15 Rules from STATUS)
- **O**pen: Distance between joints. Take up space.
- **G**rounded: Energy in body, not mind. Present.
- **S**low: Move like you're underwater. No fidgeting.
- **P**riding: Comfortable drawing attention.

**The 15 Body Language Rules:**
1. Take up more space -- open up, radiate energy outwards
2. **Crotch Gathering** -- expose phallus region (legs open, thumb in belt loop)
3. Slow down all movements. Marlon Brando's walk = "the slowest, sexiest walk" (Mary Tyler Moore)
4. Non-reactivity to noises and environment. Stay with her even if sirens go off.
5. Lean BACK most of the time. Leaning in = low status.
6. Lean against things when you can (walls = high status behavior)
7. Room in front of you, wall behind you (caveman wiring)
8. In loud bars, slowly whisper in her ear, then slowly lean right back
9. Strong eye contact when talking, ~50% when she's talking (she has to work for your attention)
10. Don't fake-laugh. If it's not funny, don't laugh. Cut this entirely.
11. Wide array of presentation when talking -- smooth, controlled, purposeful movements
12. Blink less often (Osho interviews: 30 seconds without blinking before answering)
13. Always end the conversation first. Foot trick: point one foot toward exit.
14. When sitting: legs spread, crotch out, arms spread. Thumbs in pockets or belt loop.
15. When standing: thumbs in pockets, legs spread, lean on wall with legs crossed.

- **Training:** Focus on 1 of 15 rules per day for 15 days. Become a long-term student (read Joe Navarro "What Every BODY is Saying").

### 3. VOICE (5 High Status Traits)
- **Seeking Rapport** (upward inflection) = low status, seeking approval
- **Neutral Rapport** (flat) = high status default
- **Breaking Rapport** (downward) = talking down, use sparingly -- stay Neutral to Breaking

**The 5 Traits of a High Status Voice:**
1. **Authoritative** -- expects a positive response, commands attention, loud enough for the environment
2. **Clear** -- cuts through, directed at person you're talking to, enunciates
3. **Unaffected** -- you speak YOUR way no matter who you're with. Changing your voice = low status. The person whose vocal characteristics change the least is perceived as highest status.
4. **Interesting** -- use Pattern Interrupts (faster, slower, louder, quieter, higher, deeper). Variety = intriguing.
5. **Often Breaks Rapport** -- downward tonality, high status default

- **Shock Status Trick:** When she's losing attention, say "HEY!" loud, in a down-tone high status way. Then go back to playful/light vibe.
- **Volume +20% Rule:** Practice speaking 20% louder 3x/day
- **Models:** Harvey Spectre (Suits), Samuel Jackson, Johnny Depp, Russell Brand, RDJ, Brad Pitt (Ocean's)

### 4. EYE CONTACT (The #1 Most Honest Signal)
- Eyes CANNOT lie about status. The #1 most infallible honest signal.
- Smart women will consciously look at your eyes to see if you can handle high pressure.
- **"If you can't handle looking her in the eyes, how can you handle fucking her like a man should?"**

**The 90/60 Rule:**
- When YOU talk: 80-90% eye contact (look into her soul)
- When SHE talks: 50-60% eye contact (she has to work for your attention)

**Advanced Eye Contact Techniques:**
- **The Slow Turn** -- when someone calls your name, DON'T jerk your head. Keep working, then slowly turn. (Tony Stark/Iron Man while working)
- **The Still Head** -- move your EYES, not your head. Person who moves head least = highest status.
- **The Eye Squint** -- squinting communicates breaking rapport + "I'm not sure about you yet." Use with little grin.
- **Looking away tells everything:** Low status = jerk away when caught. High status = hold eye contact, maybe smile, slowly look at something even more interesting.
- **Singular Focus Exercise:** TV on (sound off), put object on top of screen. Stare at object 60 seconds, 3 sets/day. After 2 weeks, your ability to look someone dead in the eye is superb.
- **Cold Shower Exercise:** Hot shower → cold for 60 seconds. Pick a spot and stare calmly. 3 sets. Trains tension tolerance.

### 5. WALK (5 Characteristics)
1. Walks tall and open. Shoulders back, chin up. Thumbs out or in pockets.
2. Knows where he's going. Walks purposefully. Led by dominant, positive energy.
3. Smooth and controlled. Often slower than most. Nothing makes a high status man walk at a pace he doesn't want.
4. Casually masculine stroll. Thought pattern: "I'm feeling really sexually powerful." Knowing swagger + sly grin.
5. Often looking beyond the crowd. On his path. Not looking around for approval.

**The Door-Frame Pause:** When walking into a room, pause at the doorframe. Breathe it all in. Take in the environment. This gives everyone the opportunity to see how much of a badass you are.
- **Phrase to shock yourself into walking awesome:** "Walk tall. Walk relaxed. Fuck I'm awesome."
- **Marlon Brando's walk** to his trailer -- "the slowest, sexiest walk" Mary Tyler Moore had ever seen

### 6. STATE CONTROL (6 Habits)
- A woman needs to know on a subconscious level that you can HANDLE TENSION
- Emotional reactivity kills attraction instantly
- State and Status are VERY closely related -- great state = high status sub-communications
- When you have tremendous state control, shit tests go past you "like farts in the wind"

**The 6 Habits to Develop State Control:**
1. **Always interpret high status.** She turns away during a kiss? Interpret it as she wants to kiss later, not never. Ask: "How would the high status version of me interpret this?"
2. **Respect your body.** JERF (Just Eat Real Food). Fast 24 hours once/week (Eat Stop Eat). Consistent workout program.
3. **Physical Anchoring.** Not in good state? Shake your whole body violently. Voice exercises. Shock yourself into good state.
4. **Go-To Thought Loops.** High achievers have 12-15K thoughts/day. Low achievers have 60-70K. Loop affirmations: "I love myself. Hot girls love me. Life is awesome. I'm such a fucking 12."
5. **Physical Challenges.** Cold showers (60 seconds, straight-faced like a boss). Heavy squats with straight face = practicing non-reactivity. Combine with Thought Loops.
6. **90-Day Commitment.** Make your desired state your default setting through 90 days of consistent practice.

- **The Slow Turn** -- when someone calls your name, don't look up right away. Keep working. A few seconds later, slowly turn your head. (Iron Man/Tony Stark working in his lab)
- **"If you're in a really bad state, don't resist -- accept it. Go DEEPER into it. 'I'm so sad, I'm so sad!' Then it just dissipates."**

### 7. CAREFREENESS (High Status Indifference/Detachment)
- Neediness is the #1 attraction killer
- **"We want that which does not want us. We pursue that which retreats from us."** Basic primal hardwiring.
- Coming from abundance -- you don't NEED any particular woman
- "Being pursued > being pursuer"
- **"Instead of playing hard to get, you eventually BECOME hard to get"**

**Why Indifference Triggers Attraction:**
- Implies you have lots of options (pre-selection)
- Women can't affect you emotionally → affects HER more emotionally
- She wonders if you like her → she's investing thought in you → Thought Investment Rationalization: "If I'm thinking about him this much, I must be REALLY into him"
- You become like a mystery or puzzle she has to figure out
- You're always lighter, happier, more relaxed -- the exact vibe everyone wants to be around
- **When it's clear you truly don't need someone, they cannot help but feel a magnetic pull toward you**

**True indifference = she NEVER knows if she actually has you.** You love yourself and your world so much that you could walk away and be just as good, if not better, at anytime.

- **Dean Martin example:** Lost $20k golf bet, said "Don't worry kid, I can make it back faster than you can win it"
- **Monogamy speech example:** "I don't believe in monogamy... You get my emotional connection, no other girl will have that." She leaves for 3 days. You don't call (give women space to come to the right conclusion). She calls back: "I don't care about the label, I just want to be with you."

### 8. TRUTH (High Status Truth-Giving)
- "Confidently speaking your mind" -- Jason's entire brand
- Giving truth freely is one of the hardest missions -- and because of that, it might just be the thing that makes you strongest
- **"Post-modernism humor"** -- reference the process, call everything out instead of leaving it unsaid. Don't fight for the armrest on a plane -- joke about it.
- **"Slap before the crest"** -- give truth/challenge FIRST, then the compliment. More effective than complimenting straight away.

**Why Truth Triggers Obsession:**
- Refreshingly real and rare -- stands out in a world of bullshit
- Lets her know she can trust you over all other men
- Makes you SO strong (constantly leaning into your edges, outside comfort zone)
- Unpredictable -- anything could happen next

**How to Give Truth:**
1. Start seeing truth -- search for and find it in every situation, with no judgment
2. Start saying it matter-of-factly, nonchalantly. Give truth freely.
3. Love the moments of challenge when you feel a pull NOT to say it -- run right through them

### 9. RAPPORT CONTROL (HVST + Breaking Rapport)
- **HVST (High Value Small Talk)** -- goes WAY beyond normal small talk
- **Deep rapport** = discussing/experiencing deeper emotions. **Wide rapport** = lighter, fun topics.
- **7 Rapport Topics:** Hopes, Fears, Dreams, Desires, Passions, Family/Friends, Secrets

**Best Rapport Questions:**
- "If your house was on fire and you could grab 3 things, what would you take?"
- "What was the longest relationship you've been in?"
- "Which of your friends will you still be close with 10 years from now?"
- "What was your favorite TV show as a kid?"
- "So what are you trying to do with your life?"

**Breaking Rapport (High Status Move):**
- Higher status people break rapport MORE often -- and this communicates higher status
- Breaking rapport (downward tonality, disagreeing, teasing, pushing away) entices the other person to seek rapport with YOU
- Getting mad once in a while is perceived as high status. Angry all the time = unattractive.
- **"Banish trying for rapport. Have them try for rapport with you, or have game recognize game."**

### 10. CHARISMA (UPLIE Framework)
- **"Certainty in Motion"** -- decisiveness, passion, bold action
- Charisma presupposes leadership. The one leading the tribe had the most status.
- **"A man whose relentless passion for his own vision inspires him to be intensely independent and always dominating his own path"**

**UPLIE -- The 5 Principles of Charm:**
1. **U**napologetic/Free -- speak freely across all status levels. Be a beacon of permission.
2. **P**resent -- fully at peace with this moment. Can feel where everyone is at all times.
3. **L**ight/Playful -- even when content is serious, the VIBE is not. Nothing is ever a big deal.
4. **I**nterested but Indifferent -- make her feel like the only person in the world, but she still doesn't HAVE you. (Bill Clinton's reality distortion field)
5. **E**ye Contact -- deep, powerful, relaxed. The #1 honest signal.

**Red Carpet Theory:** When talking to someone, you lay out the red carpet for them -- but YOU choose where that red carpet goes. They feel emotionally great while you lead.

### 11. STYLE (DTF System -- Dressed To Fuck)

**The DTF System (4 Steps):**
1. **Incongruency Theory** -- be the 1 orange in a lineup of 99 reds. Attention = Perceived Value.

4. **Fuck Triggers** -- accessories that create incongruity (bracelets, rings, necklaces, hats, sunglasses, tattoos, leather jacket). Max 4 triggers or you overdose it.
