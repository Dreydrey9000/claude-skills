## THE CAREFREE INSTALLATION (30-Day Program -- FULL)

Based on Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) principles. Rated 9.2/10. Jason's most transformative inner-game program.

**Day 1 Principle:** "Give less fucks & you get more fucks"

**Core Teaching:**
- Focus is within = you are golden & magnetic
- Focus is without = you are a slave
- "Pussy is not a zero-sum game" -- accept this and abundance begins
- All emotions are muscles -- strengthen carefreeness through repetition

### FAST START (Days 1-3)
1. **List everything on your mind.** Star what's in your control. Dash what's not. Focus ONLY on starred items.
2. **Attachments audit.** List everything you're attached to. Ask: Why do I want this? Who told me I should? Will it make me happy forever? *What makes you happy is the progress you make and the person you become.*
3. **Circle of Influence vs. Circle of Concern** (Steven Covey). Draw two circles. Inner circle = things you control. Outer = concerns. Only inner circle gets significance from you.

### DEFINITIONS OF CAREFREENESS (Days 4-5)
- **Carefree = positive detachment from outcomes**, in a way that's best for you and everybody else
- Your emotional state will be perpetually dominant regardless of outcomes
- You are **process focused** -- dominating each moment
- Carefree is not something you attain; it's a **decision to radically accept all reality**
- You don't "get" things -- you **attract** them. "Getting is what mediocrity does."
- **2 Types:** (1) Magnetic Carefreeness (general state) and (2) Purposeful Carefreeness (leveraging magnetism toward your BHT Vision)
- **The Mesmer Effect:** People will be mesmerized at how carefree you are

### THE ACCEPT ALL PRINCIPLE (Day 7)
- Every moment is your opportunity to accept fully
- Problems don't exist -- you're just resisting reality
- **Physical cue:** When resisting, arms close to chest. Practice opening up.
- **Doing the Opposite:** In common situations, do the opposite of what everyone does. Trains courage + CF muscle.
- **Redefine words:** Frustration = "about to have a breakthrough." Confusion = "about to learn something new."

### THE $100 TIP
When you are resisting something, your jaw clenches. When accepting, your jaw is completely relaxed. **Simply relax your jaw = trigger complete acceptance.** Mind follows body.

## THE 7 FORBIDDEN WORDS (The Obsession Code -- FULL)

These 7 words trigger the **Female Obsession Response** -- hardcoded in her DNA. When triggered, she has no control over it. The thoughts begin slowly, then pick up speed. Pretty soon, you're all she's thinking about.

### WORD #1: VISION
Your big, huge, throbbing vision. Where you're going at breakneck speed.

**Why it triggers obsession:**
- So fucking rare. Ambition feeds her primal desires for security and social status.
- Higher Status. She wants to project herself into it, envision it, invest in it mentally.
- She's always #2, trying to be #1 but will never truly get there (and never actually wants to -- then she can't trust you)
- **The Teleological Loophole** -- humans are happiest when in motion toward their vision. Having a clear vision makes you teleological 24/7 -- always "on" and unbelievably attractive.
- **"The enemy of man is not women. The enemy is COMPROMISE."** The extent of pain you feel is directly related to the amount of compromise you've made for a woman.

**How to communicate it:**
1. Find it (ongoing process -- evolves as you evolve)
2. Align your life with it (Vision-Respect: does this move me closer to my vision?)
3. Communicate from power -- don't share it all at once. Bits and pieces. Loops and tension. Light up when talking about it.

### WORD #2: TRUTH
Giving truth = calmly saying all the things most people won't. Living at the heart of the matter.

**Why it triggers obsession:** Higher status, highly masculine, indifferent, unpredictable, refreshingly real, makes you SO strong. She can trust you over all other men.

**How:** Start seeing truth → start giving it freely, nonchalantly → love the moments of challenge when you feel the pull NOT to say it. Run right through them. Giving truth should be **proactive and constructive** -- meant to challenge and raise people up.

### WORD #3: CERTAINTY
Makes her unconsciously FEEL: this is the right man to follow. I can trust this man.

**Why:** Goes places. Law of Expectations. Pre-selection (implies people already follow you). Women want a man incredibly certain about his path but **less certain about them** (more on Thought Investment).

**How to communicate:** (1) Certainty Tonality (2) Certainty in direction and movements (3) Certainty in VISION (4) No qualifying, explaining, or questioning.

**The secret about certainty:** It is completely amoral. Can serve any purpose.

### WORD #4: UNCERTAINTY
Obsession intensifies when she's thinking about you constantly, especially when you're not around.

**Thought Investment Rationalization:** "If I'm thinking about him this much, I must be REALLY into him." This is how all obsession works. This is also how "love" works for most people.

**How to leave room for uncertainty:**
1. Maintain your air of mystery. Never tell all.
2. Unpredictability/Spontaneity -- she can't control you (and never truly wants to)
3. Competition Anxiety
4. Nonchalance/Indifference
5. Random emotion rollercoaster rides (strong emotions -- good or bad -- create the same effect)
6. Takeaways/disqualifiers/leave her hanging sometimes

**The line:** If it seems on purpose, the spell is broken. Also, too much too often may be too painful to continue (but she'll likely never lose attraction -- not after all that thought investment).

### WORD #5: STATUS
The 12 Honest Signals. **#1 Law of Fucking:** Women only want to fuck guys they think are better than them. They conclude this through your sub-communications, not outward status symbols.

She is WIRED to be attracted to the man who displays most of the 12 signals. **She is OBSESSED with the guy who has all 12. Will not want to leave him, ever. Will do whatever he says.**

### WORD #6: VALIDATION
Women constantly stack themselves up socially, looking to move higher. The higher the status of the validation source, the more amplified the effect.

**How to use validation for obsession:**
1. Become a High Status Male she craves validation FROM in the first place
2. Ingrain your naturally high standards, values, and boundaries
3. Give truth freely -- which unpredictably validates/invalidates on its own
4. Purposely (usually playfully) invalidate/validate/invalidate to challenge and spark attraction

**Techniques:**
- **The Power Switch** -- flip who has the power in the interaction
- **Stating preferences** -- tell her what you like
- **Setting high bars** -- raise what she has to live up to
- **Always half-validating** -- never give full validation
- **Half-validating other girls in front of her**

**The most exciting truth:** To master validation, you must become the most VALIDATING source in your own life.

### WORD #7: DRAMATIC DESIRE
When her desire for you is wrapped up in a greater, dramatic storyline. Their coming together is bigger than just guy and girl.

**Why:** Plays into fate/fantasy myth embedded since childhood. Injects incredible addictive excitement into her otherwise mundane life. Always involves barriers, and **barriers = desire**. The barriers make sex forbidden -- and women are enraptured by the forbidden.

**How to amplify:**
1. **Storylines** -- the high-status guy everyone wants but only she earns the "real" version. She gets SOME realness over time but NEVER all of it. She doesn't actually want it -- she just wants to constantly be working towards it.
2. **Barriers** -- friends, family, other lovers, disqualifiers, your mission keeping her from you tonight
3. **Common Enemies** -- things you both strongly dislike (playful and serious)
4. **Future Projections with Barriers** -- "I want you now, your body pressed against mine... but we can't."
5. **The Forbidden** -- doing forbidden things. Usually spontaneous, spur of the moment.
