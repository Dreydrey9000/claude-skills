## LESSON 1: THE FOUNDATION

### Three Tendencies of Human Nature

```
1. People resist unwelcome attempts to persuade them
2. People can't resist what they can't detect
3. People sometimes believe what they are told, but never doubt what they conclude
```

### Two Ugly Truths

1. **We are ALL manipulators** — We all seek to influence others for our own purposes
2. **Our morality serves as a filter** — It colors our perceptions and justifies our actions

### The Ethics Test (Use Before Every Technique)

> "How would others respond if my true intentions were unexpectedly discovered?"

| Response | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Outraged, cheated, betrayed | DON'T DO IT |
| Thrilled, excited, amused | PROCEED |
| Not surprised at all | MOST COMMON — usually appreciated |

## LESSON 2: THE 7 HIDDEN ADDICTIONS

These are the psychological needs that drive ALL human behavior.

### Addiction #2: Hope

> Those who create hope in the face of hopelessness command attention. Those who deliver on their promises command loyalty.

**Application:** In copy, always paint the path from current pain → future state. Never leave them hopeless.

### Addiction #5: Know Things Others Don't

Secret knowledge is irresistible.

**Application:**
- "What they don't tell you about..."
- "The insider secret to..."
- "What I'm about to share isn't public..."
- Frame your knowledge as exclusive

## LESSON 3: ATTENTION CAPTURE

### The Achilles Heel

> We may never know our attention has been captured until after it is released.

### 4 Effects of Captured Attention

1. **Paralysis** — Fixed, unable to look away
2. **Approach** — Move toward what captures us
3. **Matching** — Begin to mirror or align with it
4. **Obedience** — Follow its suggestions

## LESSON 4: PSYCHOLOGICAL VENTRILOQUISM

### The Core Principle

> Assumptions enter our consciousness unevaluated and unlabeled as such and appear indistinguishable from fact.

### SAY vs. CONVEY

| Type | When to Use | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| SAY | Verifiable facts | "We've been in business 20 years" |
| CONVEY | Opinions/judgments | (Show technical expertise, don't claim it) |

### The Rule

- **SAY** facts that can be verified
- **CONVEY** things that would sound arrogant or unbelievable if stated directly

### Show, Don't Tell

Like filmmakers: "The bad guy walks in and you know he's bad — but not from dialogue."

**Application:**
- Don't say "I'm an expert" → Show expertise through specifics
- Don't say "This is valuable" → Stack proof until they conclude it
- Don't say "You can trust me" → Be transparent until they trust you

### The Comedian's Secret

Lead audience to make false assumptions, then reveal them. The humor comes from recognizing their own assumption.

**Example:** "My grandmother has been walking 3 miles a day for 5 years. Now we don't know where the hell she is."

We assumed "exercise" — the punchline reveals our assumption.

### The Magician's Secret

- Rolling up sleeves → CONVEYS nothing hidden
- Running a hoop around assistant → CONVEYS no wires
- Never SAY "There's nothing up my sleeve" — SHOW it

## LESSON 6: CMAD FRAMEWORK

## THE ONE SENTENCE PERSUASION COURSE

The complete formula in one sentence:

> "People will do anything for those who encourage their dreams, justify their failures, allay their fears, confirm their suspicions, and help them throw rocks at their enemies."

## CONTEXT OF POWER

### 5 Traits of Those Who Command Authority

1. **Attribute Knowledge to Mysterious Sources** — Don't claim brilliance, claim to understand brilliant sources
2. **Be a Pattern-Finder** — Those who identify patterns in chaos become trusted
3. **Be a Label-Maker** — Create names for experiences and phenomena
4. **Give Others a Sense of Power** — "The decision is yours" — even when guiding them
5. **Be Difficult to Reach/Understand** — "There is no long line to the wise man at the bottom of the mountain"

## COPY FORMULAS

### Formula 1: The Scapegoat Lead
```
Can't [achieve result]?
It may not be your fault.
[Scapegoat] has been working against you.
Here's what's really going on...
```

### Formula 2: The Secret Knowledge Hook
```
What [authority figures] don't want you to know about [topic]...
[Insider information]
This changes everything about how you [action].
```

### Formula 3: The "You Were Right" Opener
```
You've always suspected [their suspicion].
You were right.
[Validation + Proof]
Here's what to do about it...
```

### Formula 4: The Enemy Frame
```
[Enemy] has been [harmful action].
That's why [negative result they've experienced].
It's time to [action against enemy].
```
