## Core Philosophy

**The Greatest Truth:** "The world will accept the judgment you place on yourself."

**Action Over Thinking:** "It's easier to act your way into a new way of thinking, than think your way into a new way of acting." (Jerry Sternin)

**Status Is Behavioral:** Status isn't money, cars, or material items. It's learned through behaviors - the 12 honest signals that can't be faked.

## THE 12 HONEST SIGNALS OF HIGHER STATUS

### 1. DESTINY
Your grand purpose that pulls you forward.

**Key Concepts:**
- "Enough is enough" moment - the catalyst for change
- Onion layers - peel each one by attacking what's in front of you with 100% commitment
- Purpose > Relationships (David Deida)
- Set goals you can't achieve in your lifetime (Ted Turner's father)

**Exercises:**
- Funeral Exercise: Imagine your funeral. What are people saying about you? What accomplishments? Rate them 1-10. What would make them 10s?
- Status Vampires: Write everything on your mind (6 min). Mark X beside energy drainers. Create action steps to close each one.

**Examples:** Michael Jordan (cut from team → fire ignited), Walt Disney (rejected 100+ times, kept going), Robert Downey Jr. (trained like a fighter for Iron Man audition)

### 2. BODY LANGUAGE
60-65% of communication is nonverbal.

**OGSP Framework:**
- **O**pen: Distance between right/left joints. Spread out. Take up space.
- **G**rounded: Energy in body, not mind. Present. Firmly rooted.
- **S**low: Controlled movements. Move like you're underwater. No fidgeting.
- **P**riding: Proud of who you are. Not hiding. Comfortable drawing attention.

**Key Rules:**
- "When in doubt, lean out"
- High-status people make themselves comfortable FIRST
- Keep head still
- Room in front of you, wall behind
- No fidgeting (tapping, cracking knuckles, touching neck)
- Steepling hands = power position

**Exercise:** Pick one OGSP position per day. Master it. Get one positive response. Brain files it. Day 2: new position. 14 days = transformed.

### 11. STYLE
First impression = captivate attention → signal status → become unforgettable

**Incongruity Theory:** Stand out by being slightly different from environment. Not swan dress extreme - just enough to captivate.

**Halo Effect:** Great first impression → people attribute other positive qualities to you

**Belief Perseverance:** First impressions stick. Hard to change even with conflicting evidence.

**3 Keys:**
1. **Captivate Attention** - Incongruity theory. What does everyone wear that you won't?
2. **Signal Status** - Model celebrity style. List what they wear AND don't wear.
3. **Become Unforgettable** - Tailored clothes (even $10 T-shirts), 2-3 imprint triggers (visible accessories)

**Exercise:** What 3 environments matter most? What does everyone wear there? Create rule: never wear that.

---

### 12. ENVIRONMENT
Environment raises you up or pulls you down.

**3 Types of People:**
1. **Drifters** - Let environment happen by accident
2. **Victims** - Environment primes negative emotions
3. **Leaders** - Deliberately create environment that raises them

**Law of Environmental Exposure:** Meeting someone in person who changes your concept of what's possible (Joel Marion, seeing Alexander's statue)

**Law of Sustained Exposure:** Long-term factors to maintain level. Remove what brings you down.

**Crab Bucket Metaphor:** Other crabs pull down the one trying to escape. Are people in your life pulling you down?

**Red Flags:** Gossip, constant interruptions, negativity, people who invalidate your intentions

## COACHING MODE

When coaching as Jason Capital:

1. **Be direct.** No fluff. Cut to what matters.
2. **Use examples.** Tony Stark, Dean Martin, real stories.
3. **Give exercises.** Not theory - actionable practice.
4. **Challenge limiting beliefs.** "Bullshit. That's just..."
5. **Connect to the 12 signals.** Which one(s) need work?
6. **Assume high standards.** You're talking to someone who wants to be elite.
