# Style Profile: @ryanmagin (Ryan Magin — Viral Editz CEO)

## Source
- Reference: https://www.instagram.com/reel/DJ26gjxRtRs/
- Creator: @ryanmagin
- Platform: Instagram Reels
- Duration: 43s
- Resolution: 720x1280
- Date analyzed: 2026-02-02

## Subtitle Style
- **Type:** Word-by-word highlight with DUAL alternating colors (yellow/green)
- **Font:** Poppins Black Italic (closest match to reference)
- **Size:** ~62pt at 1080p (scales with resolution)
- **Case:** ALL CAPS, no punctuation
- **Normal color:** #FFFFFF (white)
- **Highlight color 1:** #FFE600 (warm yellow)
- **Highlight color 2:** #00E600 (bright lime green)
- **Alternation:** Colors alternate per word display window
- **Stroke:** Black (#000000), ~5px at 1080p — NO background pill
- **Shadow:** None (stroke only)
- **Position:** Mid-frame center (Y: ~48% of frame height) — NOT lower third
- **Layout:** Max 2 lines, ~3 words per line
- **Behavior:** Active spoken word/line gets colored, rest stays white

## Overlay Style
- **Product/article images:** Appear in lower half of frame when mentioned
- **Width:** ~75% of frame width
- **Corners:** Slightly rounded (~12px radius)
- **Red arrow:** Points to key items in overlays
- **Sourced from:** Official product pages, Google Shopping results, brand sites
- **Examples from reference:** Walmart governor listing, ChipYourCar.com performance chips, Ferrari dashboard, car manufacturer logos grid, iPhone mockup, Xbox TV-in-car

## Zoom & Camera
- **Pattern:** Alternates between zoomed out (full seated body) and zoomed in (chest-up punch-in ~1.3x)
- **Trigger:** Zoom changes on emphasis moments and cut points
- **Interval:** ~3 seconds average

## Pacing
- **Average cut interval:** ~2.5 seconds
- **Style:** Fast hard cuts, no transitions
- **Energy:** High — sentences broken into short subtitle bursts

## Color Grade
- **Temperature:** Neutral to cool (studio LED lighting, acoustic panel room)
- **Contrast:** High
- **Saturation:** Normal
- **Vignette:** Slight natural from studio lighting

## Set Design (for reference, not cloned)
- Dark studio with acoustic foam panels
- LED color-changing lights under sneakers/chair
- Warm industrial lighting (left side)
- Sticker-covered panels (upper corners)
