## Locked Styles

### Style 1: PODCAST (Primary — Most Used)

**The go-to style.** Dark, moody podcast studio. Proven in 7+ approved generations.

| Element | Setting |
|---------|---------|
| **Setting** | Podcast studio |
| **Background** | Dark concrete/stone wall, industrial |
| **Chair** | Teal/emerald velvet chair or couch |
| **Microphone** | Shure SM7B on boom arm |
| **Headphones** | Around neck or on desk |
| **Lighting** | Warm key light from left, dramatic, moody. Dark ambient with rim light |
| **Atmosphere** | Blurred fairy lights or LED strips in far background |
| **Framing** | Medium close-up, chest up. Cinematic shallow DOF |
| **Style** | Candid podcast moment, not posed. Like a screencap of the best interview moment |

**Default Wardrobe Options:**
- Black blazer (classic — most used)
- Cream knit sweater (cozy authority)
- Sage green top
- White fitted top

**Default Poses/Actions:**
- Mid-laugh, genuine smile showing teeth
- One hand up gesturing while talking
- Hand on chest (just told a funny story)
- Looking at mic while smiling (candid moment)
- Direct eye contact, confident smirk

**Prompt Template (Podcast):**
```
[Identity Anchor] She's in the podcast studio — dark concrete walls, teal velvet chair,
Shure SM7B microphone on boom arm to her right. She is [POSE/ACTION]. Wearing [WARDROBE],
hair [HAIR STYLE]. [HEADPHONES if applicable]. LIGHTING: Dramatic warm key light from the
left. Dark moody atmosphere. Blurred fairy lights in far background. FRAMING: Medium
close-up from chest up. Cinematic shallow DOF. Style: candid podcast moment, not posed.
Like someone screencapped a real interview at the best moment.
```
