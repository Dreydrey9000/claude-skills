# Product Examples: Low-Ticket Breakdowns

## Successful Product Template

Every low-ticket product follows this pattern:

**Problem:** ONE specific pain point
**Promise:** Clear result in specific timeframe
**Structure:** 10-12 videos, under 90 minutes total
**Price:** $17-$97 (usually $37-$47)
**Bonus:** Cheat sheet or workbook (Canva)

---

## Example 1: 1000 Instagram Followers in 30 Days

**The Narrowing:**
- Level 1: Social media
- Level 2: Instagram growth
- Level 3: Get 1,000 followers in 30 days

**Why It Works:**
- Specific number (1,000)
- Specific timeframe (30 days)
- Single platform (Instagram)
- Achievable for beginners

**NOT:** "Master social media" or "Grow your online presence"

## Example 3: Passive Income with Instagram

**The Model Course Structure:**

## Product Naming Formula

**Template:** "[Specific Result] in [Specific Timeframe]"

**Good Examples:**
- 1000 Instagram Followers in 30 Days
- $5K Month Without Sales Calls
- 21 Meals in 8 Minutes Each
- First 5 Clients in 14 Days

**Bad Examples:**
- Ultimate Social Media Guide
- Health Transformation Program
- Business Success Blueprint
- Complete Marketing System

---

## What Makes Products Sell

1. **Specific promise** - Numbers, timeframes, clear outcome
2. **Believable result** - Not too good to be true
3. **Low barrier** - Price anyone can afford
4. **Quick win potential** - They can start seeing results fast
5. **Solves active pain** - Something they're Googling at 2am
6. **One thing** - Not a overwhelming course

## What Kills Sales

1. **Vague promises** - "Transform your life"
2. **Too much content** - 47 modules nobody completes
3. **Wrong price** - Too high for the transformation
4. **No urgency** - Why buy today vs. someday?
5. **Unclear outcome** - What do I actually get?
6. **Generic solution** - Could apply to anyone
