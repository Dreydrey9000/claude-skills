# Traffic Strategy: Reels + Carousels

## The Core Equation

```
REELS = TRAFFIC (bring in non-followers)
CAROUSELS = NURTURE (convert followers to buyers)
```

You need BOTH. They work together symbiotically.

## Reels: Your Traffic Foot Soldiers

**What They Do:**
- Go out and find new people
- Bring non-followers to your profile
- Create massive reach (300K+ potential per reel)
- Feed the algorithm

**Real Numbers Example:**
- One reel: 300K views
- Reached ~10K followers
- Reached ~300K NON-followers
- That's 300K new eyeballs from one piece of content

**Why Reels Feel Hard:**
Most people tense up about reels. Maria did too. The key is having a simple, repeatable system.

**Easy Reels You Can Do:**
- Talking head with one tip
- Screen share teaching something
- Faceless reels (hands doing something with voiceover)
- B-roll with text overlay
- Quick tutorial or how-to

**Faceless Options:**
365 faceless reel ideas exist for people who don't want face on camera:
- Filming hands doing a task
- Screen recordings with voiceover
- Text-based reels with music
- B-roll compilations

## Carousels: Your Nurture Machine

**What They Do:**
- Turn strangers into buyers
- Build relationship and trust
- Create conversation in comments
- Drive purchases (spikes happen after carousels)

**Real Numbers Example:**
- Carousel: 7K reach (much less than reels)
- BUT: Direct correlation to sales spikes
- Comments are questions and engagement
- Creates actual connection

**Why Both Matter:**
```
Reels without carousels = strangers who never buy
Carousels without reels = no new people seeing them
```

## Creating Carousels (Ultra-Simple Method)

**Process (4 minutes):**
1. Open Notes app on phone
2. Write 10 slides of content (tips, steps, insights)
3. Add padding (press enter) at top and bottom of each slide
4. Screenshot each slide (wait for gray scroll bar to disappear)
5. Go to Instagram → Post → Select carousel images
6. Center each image in the frame
7. Add swipe finger emoji 👉 to encourage scrolling
8. Post or schedule

**Content Ideas:**
- Educational tips (numbered lists)
- Step-by-step processes
- Common mistakes
- Quick wins
- Behind-the-scenes insights

**Best Practices:**
- Always 10 slides
- Use pointing emojis 👉 to indicate swipe
- First slide = hook/title
- Last slide = CTA
- Simple text on clean background
- No fancy design needed

## Posting Frequency

**Ideal State (work up to this):**
- 1 reel per day
- 1 carousel per day
- Pitch in stories daily

**Starting Out:**
- Don't burn yourself out
- Build up slowly and sustainably
- Better to be consistent at lower volume than crash at high volume

**Signs You're at Sustainable Pace:**
- You enjoy creating content
- You're ahead of schedule
- You have content queued for next week
- It feels genuinely manageable

## The 90-Day Challenge

For maximum results:
- Pitch in stories every single day for 90 days
- Link to checkout page
- Consistent call to action
- Track what happens

**Why 90 Days:**
- Builds the habit
- Gives algorithm time to learn your content
- Creates enough data to see what works
- Most people give up too early

## Content That Drives Sales

**Brag + Pitch Formula:**

Every 3 posts, include:
1. **Educational value** (tips, teaching)
2. **Brag** (results, testimonials, wins)
3. **Pitch** (direct CTA to product)

**Finding Your Brags:**
- Student results
- Personal milestones
- Revenue numbers (if comfortable)
- Lifestyle wins
- Consistency streaks

## Stories Strategy

**Daily Story Sequence:**
1. Personal/behind-the-scenes content
2. Value/tip related to your offer
3. Pitch with link to checkout

**Pro Tips:**
- Always include link to checkout
- Use countdown stickers for urgency
- Share testimonials/results
- Show income proof (if appropriate)
- Make it feel natural, not salesy

## Traffic Metrics That Matter

**For Reels:**
- Non-follower reach (main metric)
- Saves and shares (algorithm signals)
- Profile visits (traffic to bio)

**For Carousels:**
- Comments and questions
- Saves (for reference)
- DMs received
- Sales correlation (track timing)

**Vanity Metrics (less important):**
- Like counts
- Follower count (slower growth from carousels)
- Views on low-reach posts

## Alternative Traffic: Facebook Groups

**Only use if:**
- Instagram strategy isn't working after 90 days
- Your audience is primarily on Facebook
- You need a quick win while building Instagram

**Method:**
1. Find Facebook groups in your industry
2. Check if promos are allowed
3. Create simple image post + short text
4. Clear CTA to checkout page
5. Reply to comments with link

**Results:** Much more manual than Instagram, lower scale (~24 real leads from 48 comments)

**Stick to Instagram** - reels scale infinitely with less manual work

## Common Mistakes

❌ Only posting reels (no nurture, no sales)
❌ Only posting carousels (no new audience)
❌ Inconsistent posting
❌ Not pitching in stories
❌ Linking to linktree instead of checkout
❌ Giving up before 90 days
❌ Overcomplicating content creation
