## Sales Page Copy Templates

### Headline Formulas

Pick one:
- "[Specific Result] in [Timeframe] Without [Pain Point]"
- "How to [Outcome] in [Timeframe] (Even If [Objection])"
- "The [Number]-Step System to [Result] Without [Thing They Hate]"
- "Finally: [Outcome] Without [Pain Point] or [Pain Point]"

### Subheadline Formulas

- "The simple [X]-step system to [outcome] even if [you're a beginner/you've failed before/you have no time]"
- "Learn the exact method that [credibility statement - number of people helped, results achieved]"
- "Stop [painful activity] and start [desired activity] in just [timeframe]"

### Opening Hook (2-3 sentences)

Template:
"You've tried [FAILED APPROACH 1]. You've tried [FAILED APPROACH 2]. And you're still [STUCK IN PROBLEM STATE]. It's not your fault—[REASON IT'S NOT THEIR FAULT]. There's a better way."

### The Problem Section

Template:
"Here's what [TARGET AUDIENCE] deals with every day:

- [PAIN POINT 1 - specific daily frustration]
- [PAIN POINT 2 - emotional toll]
- [PAIN POINT 3 - consequence of not solving]

You know you should [DESIRED BEHAVIOR], but [OBSTACLE]. You've probably tried [COMMON FAILED SOLUTIONS], but [WHY THEY DIDN'T WORK].

What if there was a simpler way?"

### What's Inside Section

Template:
"Here's exactly what you're getting:

✓ Video 1: [Title] - [One specific outcome they'll have after watching]
✓ Video 2: [Title] - [One specific outcome]
[Continue for all 10]

PLUS: [Bonus Name] - [What it is and why it helps]

Total value: $[INFLATED NUMBER] - Yours today for just $[ACTUAL PRICE]"

### This Is For You If Section

Template:
"This is for you if:
• You're struggling with [SPECIFIC PAIN POINT]
• You've tried [FAILED APPROACH] and it didn't work
• You want [DESIRED OUTCOME] without [THING THEY HATE]
• You're ready to [COMMITMENT REQUIRED]

This is NOT for you if:
• You're looking for [UNREALISTIC EXPECTATION]
• You're not willing to [MINIMUM EFFORT REQUIRED]"

### Price Reveal

Template:
"Get instant access to [PRODUCT NAME] for just $[PRICE]

That's less than [RELATABLE COMPARISON - a dinner out, one hour with a consultant, etc.]

And unlike [THAT THING], this pays for itself when you [FIRST SMALL WIN]."

### Guarantee

Template:
"Try it risk-free for 30 days. Go through the entire course, implement what you learn. If you don't [SPECIFIC MEASURABLE RESULT], email me and I'll refund every penny. No questions asked."
