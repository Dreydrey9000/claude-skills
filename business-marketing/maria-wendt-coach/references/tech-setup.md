# Tech Setup Guide

## Overview

Keep tech simple. Two main platforms needed, one optional enhancement.

## Kajabi (Course Hosting)

**Why Kajabi:**
- Best customer experience for courses
- Clean interface for students
- Handles all course delivery automatically
- Free trial available - make money during trial, then pay

**Course Structure in Kajabi:**
- 10-12 video lessons total
- Optional: Add Canva-designed workbook/cheatsheet as bonus
- No fancy intros needed - can add later via Fiverr (~$100) if desired

**What It Looks Like:**
- Module list with video titles
- Video player with your face or screenshare
- Progress tracking for students
- Mobile-friendly delivery

## SamCart (Checkout Pages)

**Why SamCart over Kajabi checkout:**
- Dramatically higher conversion rates
- Better design flexibility
- Checkout page converting at 6% vs 7% = $100K difference at scale

**Key Features:**
- Two-column checkout design (info left, payment right)
- Order bump capability (can double average order)
- Same monthly price regardless of products or customers
- Under $100/month

**SamCart → Kajabi Integration:**
1. Go to Integrations in SamCart
2. New Rule → E-Commerce → Product Purchased (NOT "Order Completed")
3. Choose Kajabi integration → Grant Offer
4. Select your Kajabi product
5. Save

**Checkout Page Elements:**
- Product image (create in Canva)
- Clear headline with result + price
- Bullet points of what's included
- Testimonials (3-5)
- Order bump (+$10-17 for bonus item)
- Clean payment form

## Proof (Social Proof Popups)

**What It Does:**
Displays "Jessica from Austin just purchased!" notifications on checkout page

**Why It Matters:**
- Significantly increases conversion
- Shows product is popular
- Creates FOMO
- One test without Proof showed dramatically lower conversions

**Setup:**
- Go to useproof.com
- Add small code snippet to top of SamCart page
- Configure to pull from your SamCart orders
- Very simple installation

## Link in Bio Setup

**Critical Rule:** Link DIRECTLY to checkout page

**Wrong approach:**
- Linktree with multiple options
- Generic "link in bio" style page
- Anything that adds clicks before purchase

**Right approach:**
- Single link to your main product checkout
- OR simple sales page that leads to checkout
- Sales page optional for single product (checkout page sufficient)

**Sales Page (Advanced/Optional):**
- Only needed if selling multiple products
- Long-form with testimonials, FAQs, proof
- Leads to SamCart checkout
- Requires third technology (landing page builder)
- Skip until you have multiple products

## Tech Problem-Solving Method

When stuck on tech:

1. **Set 8-minute timer** - Don't let tech become a rabbit hole
2. **Search:** "[Platform] how to [task] step by step"
3. **Add "step by step"** to get better results
4. **Play relaxing music** while troubleshooting
5. **If timer runs out:** Contact platform's chat support directly
6. **Pro tip:** If regular support slow, find their sales team - they respond faster

## Recording Setup

**Equipment needed:**
- Webcam (built-in laptop fine)
- Basic mic (Yeti recommended, iPhone headphones work)
- Natural lighting (window in front of you)

**Software options:**
- Ecamm (Mac)
- OBS (Free, any platform)
- Zoom (Record to cloud or local)

**iPad Screen Recording (if showing screen):**
- Share screen + add face overlay
- Tutorial available in passive income course
